OVERVIEW
Project Overview
Recruitment teams often face challenges managing large applicant pools, repetitive screening, and coordination gaps across departments. Our objective was to build a unified SaaS platform that streamlines the hiring process.

I developed Stratum 9 InnerView, an AI-powered recruitment system that streamlines job creation, resume screening, interview scheduling, and candidate evaluation on a round-to-round basis. The platform generates AI-based job descriptions, ranks candidates by skill match, and enables collaborative review and non-biased feedback through an integrated dashboard, ensuring a smooth and transparent hiring process.

Built using React.js, Node.js, and MySQL, and hosted on AWS EC2, Stratum 9 InnerView delivers the scalability, security, and accessibility of a modern SaaS product. By combining automation with human judgment, it empowers hiring teams to identify the best candidates efficiently while maintaining a non-biased oversight and accountability at every stage.

Key Features


01.
Smarter Job Creation with AI-Powered Precision
Stratum 9 InnerView lets hiring teams create job descriptions in minutes through AI-assisted uploads or manual entry. The AI evaluates each JD and recommends career-based, role-specific, and culture-specific skills. Managers can refine requirements, track active roles, applications, and interviews through a unified dashboard, ensuring every job post is accurate, data-driven, and organized.

02.
AI Resume Evaluation and Effortless Interview Scheduling
The platform’s AI engine carries out a non-biased screening of resumes, ranks candidates by job role compatibility, and saves hours of manual work. Hiring managers can also view applications, archive profiles, or screen via AI from manually uploaded documents. Once candidates are shortlisted, interviews can be scheduled via integrated calendars for smooth coordination.

03.
Real-Time, Structured, and Insightful In-Person Interviews
During live sessions, interviewers use AI-generated, skill-based pre-interview questions. InnerView leverages non-biased AI for real-time behavioral analysis, assessing professionalism, tone, and clarity. It can detect new skills mid-conversation and instantly suggest follow-up questions through pop-up prompts. After the interview, hiring teams can share AI-generated assessments or edit questionnaires, blending structured insights with adaptive, conversational intelligence.

04.
Data-Driven Feedback and Transparent Post-Interview Scoring
Stratum 9 InnerView generates detailed, AI-backed evaluations showing job compatibility scores, interviewer details, and insights. Profiles display “Hire” or “Low Match Probability” options, scores out of 9 for traits like work ethic, strengths, gaps, and skill success probability with graphical overviews. Managers can auto-generate offer letters via StratumGPT, download reports, and track acceptance statuses seamlessly.

05.
One-Line Script to Convert Website Traffic into Applicants
Install a one-line S9 script on your website to instantly route job seekers into the exact role pages inside Stratum 9 InnerView. Automate applicant capture, reduce drop-offs, and ensure every candidate enters your structured, AI-driven evaluation pipeline seamlessly.

06.
Consent-Based Ethical Recording with Dual-Sided AI Feedback
Stratum 9 InnerView requires candidates to provide explicit recording consent before any interview begins, ensuring ethical data use. Once the session ends, the platform generates structured, non-biased AI insights and shares them with the interviewer. This creates transparent evaluation, clarifies decision rationale, and supports continuous skill improvement for both sides.
